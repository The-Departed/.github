### <img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/iY8CRBdQXODJSC5GnV/giphy.gif" width="30">  Who We Are

**The Departed** is a collective of engineers and researchers leaving traditional methodologies behind to forge new paths in Artificial Intelligence and Sustainable Energy. We don't just analyze data; we force it to reveal its hidden logic.

Our work bridges the gap between **high-level reasoning** and **low-level physical estimation**.
